DNS, ISP, VPN, and other service blocking is used to increase security for personal computers and IoT devices by reducing the number of openings on the local network. This list is only designed for multiple servers or nodes to defend against outside attacks without needing to spend a lot of money on network security. It's recommended to make your own whitelist because everyone has their own reason to unblock some websites due to certain scenarios.

Note: Some of the lists have "Temp," which means they were previously unavailable and we don't know when they will be restored. I used the Wayback Machine to get the file before the owner or website took it down.
